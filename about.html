About Us

York Street Music Fest was born from a simple idea: bring world-class live music to the heart of Denver. What started as a neighborhood block party has grown into a multi-stage, multi-day festival celebrating artists across hip-hop, indie rock, electronic, and jazz. We believe music should be accessible, electric, and community-driven — and every year we work to make that a reality on York Street.

Where at

We're set up right in the heart of Denver's RiNo Art District on York Street. Easily accessible by light rail, bike, or car — come find us and stay a while.
